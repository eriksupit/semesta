## Video Explainer Semesta Digital *(working title)*
*Draft v1.0 · 8 Juni 2026*

---

## Reading Guide

Each row represents one segment from the D-016 blueprint. Columns:

- **Seg** — Segment number per D-016 revised blueprint
- **Duration** — Target duration in seconds
- **Format** — Production type (AI platform / Live / Post-prod)
- **Shot Type** — Camera framing and movement
- **Character / Subject** — Who or what is on screen
- **Action** — What happens visually
- **VO Text** — Word-for-word voice over (if any). "—" = no VO this segment
- **UI Element on Screen** — What device/UI is visible (if any)
- **Mood / Lighting** — Color palette and emotional tone
- **Music Note** — Scoring state at this segment

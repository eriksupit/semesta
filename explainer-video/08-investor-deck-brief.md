## Section 4 — Character One-Liners

**S2 Ibu RT:** The informal economy's first proof point — a home seller's first transaction indistinguishable in dignity from anyone else's.
**S4 Pemuda:** Skills and knowledge that have always existed in communities, now accessible through the same system that pays for goods.
**S6 Kurir:** Every delivery carries a transparent earnings record, no different in visibility from a corporate transaction.
**S8 Profesional F:** Productive work happens where the rules are clear — not where the office building is.
**S10 Pengusaha:** A mid-scale business that grows beyond its local market through a system that applies the same rules to the largest and smallest accounts.
**S12 Tokoh:** Trust built over a lifetime, extended through a platform whose operating principles earned the right to carry it.
**S12b Tokoh — Jeda Ibadah:** A platform that never forces a choice between livelihood and faith — the system pauses with the user when worship calls.
**S14 Eksekutif:** A corporate partner operating under the same fairness engine as the platform's smallest participant.
**S14b Ibu Belajar Kuliner:** The education vertical reaching the most underserved learner — a middle-aged home cook turning an in-app masterclass into a sellable business, every age included.
**S17b Penutup (F):** The human payoff of the closing principle — under one fair set of rules, there is a place for everyone; belonging by design, not by exception.

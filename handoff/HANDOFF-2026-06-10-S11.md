## LOCKED STANDARDS (apply to every remaining scene without being re-told)

### Tone ‚Äî single deck standard (MUTLAK)
Every `color_grade` = `Roger Deakins palette, lifted blacks medium gray, cyan-teal shadows, clean whites, Kodak 2383 emulation, cool midtones desaturated, cool-dominant zero amber push`. Reference image: `S2-karakter-1-ibu-rumah-tangga.png`.
- `slightly warmer midtones` and `neutral cool midtones` are DEPRECATED ‚Äî never use.
- Warm tokens in OTHER fields also drift the palette: `warm ambient bounce`, `warm pool`, `golden pool`, `maximum warm` ‚Üí neutralise to `cool-neutral` / `neutral pool` / `bright cool`. (Safe exceptions: `warm directness`/`warm domestic register` in `mood`, `warm expressive` in `facial_features` ‚Äî emotion/expression register, not colour.)
- Judge tone by opening the previously-approved PNG and comparing side by side ‚Äî not per-scene in isolation.
- All 6 remaining prompts have ALREADY been rolled to the cool standard in `08`. (S17's intentional "warm arrival" was cooled too ‚Äî **open question for user: restore controlled warm closer for S17, or keep cool uniform.** Default = keep cool.)

### Composition ‚Äî no dead-center clich√©
- Live-actor full body: never `subject centered frame` + eye-level. Push to right/left-third, low camera to one side, environment recedes diagonal as leading lines (validated S3).
- Even intimate MCU (S13): off-center slightly (`subject just off-center` + `slight three-quarter soft off-axis`) ‚Äî keeps intimacy, avoids dead-center (validated S9).
- Character interaction scenes: over-the-shoulder / off-axis, camera to one side (validated S4 OTS, S6 low-angle three-quarter).

### People / interaction
- Non-presenter (character) scenes = MUST be an interaction with >1 person (a moment happening between people, not a solo pose). Interaction can be co-present OR remote-via-screen (video-call second face is valid, validated S8).
- Presenter (live-actor) scenes: populate + vary the environment (glass-partition team floor, breakout floor, populated bokeh), NOT a solo empty room with `calm professional focus`. Presenter continuity = fixed wardrobe/face, NOT the same room ‚Äî vary the room.
- Background must be explicitly populated (how many people, doing what, where) in `scene_depth.background`.

### Expression ‚Äî physical only, never artificial
- No emotion labels, no narrative/meta labels (`‚Äî half-second after a sentence lands`, `‚Äî making a point`, `closest in the entire arc`).
- `brows lifted` / `eyes wide` alone read as worry/shock ‚Äî for positive register use `cheeks lifted Duchenne pull` / `cheeks lightly lifted` / `relaxed warm gaze` / `eyes crinkling`.
- Pose/gesture natural, not stiff (avoid `both palms flat arms locked`); one hand gesturing + (for female) `feminine hip displacement` / `feminine wrist tilt`.

### Graphic super
- Mandatory every scene; investor register (value proposition, not ad/user metrics; never internal term "5 Vertikal").
- Super text UNIQUE per scene ‚Äî never duplicate another scene's element 1/2 (validated S5).
- Teknik 2 frosted card via `on_screen_graphic` + `graphic_proximity` (CSS positioning: anchor + pixel offset + dimensions + z-index). For tight framings use frame-relative anchor (`right-third of frame, chest level`), not body parts that get cropped.

### Mechanical-object + hand defects (recurring text-to-image weakness)
- Vehicle handlebar/mirror shatters when a phone screen UI is forced on a bar mount ‚Üí use `phone rear casing toward camera screen hidden`, put data on a graphic card ABOVE the phone, add `simple clean minimal detail` to the bar (validated S6).
- Hands melt at contact with small objects (`hand near phone mount`) ‚Üí move hand to a neutral spot (`resting relaxed on thigh`). Minimise hand + small-object + mechanical-surface contact points per frame.

### Token discipline
- Token strings only ‚Äî no full sentences, no conjunctions, no copula, no tenses, no negations.
- No redundancy within a field (one load-bearing token), no redundancy across fields.
- Do NOT change the 6-layer prompt structure.
